## Featured Projects

### Customer Lifecycle Transformation (2023)
**Problem:** Onboarding workflows were operationally fragmented, heavily manual, and couldn't scale with rapid customer growth.

**What I built:**
- Redesigned onboarding across Operations, Product, Engineering, and Customer Success
- Implemented automation and standardized execution frameworks
- Introduced KPI visibility and operational accountability layers

**Result:** Onboarding cycle 110 days → <35 days. Measurable improvement in customer time-to-value.

### Operational Automation & Scalability (2023–2025)
**Problem:** Rapid headcount and customer growth created operational strain across servicing, workflow execution, and internal delivery.

**What I built:**
- Identified and automated high-friction workflows across the operation
- Redesigned processes to reduce manual dependency end-to-end
- Improved orchestration and operational visibility across distributed teams

### Customer Retention & Service Performance (2024–2025)
**Problem:** Organization needed to improve customer experience and consistency while scaling volume rapidly across 10,000+ employer clients and 185,000+ account holders.

**What I built:**
- Established KPI governance and performance management frameworks
- Structured customer touchpoints, review cadences, and retention-focused servicing workflows
- Introduced self-service tooling and automated forms infrastructure
- Cross-functional UX and workflow modernization to reduce operational friction
- Board and investor reporting on operational performance, compliance, and growth metrics

**Result:** GRR sustained at 93%. Churn 10% → 6.87%. CSAT 3.3 → 4.2. SLA attainment 87%+. Drove business to free cash flow positive.

### Compliance & Governance Infrastructure (2019–2025)

**What I built:**
- Designed SSAE18/SOC1/SOC2 governance frameworks from the ground up

- Built regulatory controls that scaled alongside 2x+ organizational growth
- Partnered with Product and Engineering to rebuild internal tooling infrastructure; consolidated vendor platforms to reduce annual software spend

**Result:** Achieved and sustained SOC1/SOC2/SSAE18 readiness across a regulated ERISA platform — directly enabling enterprise client acquisition.
